# Off-Balance Sheet Items Report

<Cards>
  <Card title="View Interactive Example" href="/example/off-balance-sheet-report" description="See this report with live data from the FDIC API, including searchable tables and interactive filtering" />
</Cards>

This standard report provides information on off-balance sheet items for active FDIC-insured institutions, excluding non-chartered institutions.

## Report Components

The report includes:

- **Derivatives** - Derivative contracts and their notional values
- **Total Unused Commitments** - Unused credit commitments and lines of credit

## API Request

### Endpoint

```
GET /api/financials
```

### Example Request

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=CERT,RSSDHCR,NAMEFULL,CITY,STALP,ZIP,REPDTE,BKCLASS,NAMEHCR,OFFDOM,SPECGRP,SUBCHAPS,ESTYMD,INSDATE,EFFDATE,MUTUAL,PARCERT,TRUST,REGAGNT,INSAGNT1,FDICDBS,FDICSUPV,FLDOFF,FED,OCCDIST,OTSREGNM,OFFOA,CB,OBSDIR,NACDIR,CTDERGTY,CTDERBEN,RT,RTNVS,RTFFC,RTWOC,RTPOC,FX,FXNVS,FXFFC,FXSPOT,FXWOC,FXPOC,EDCM,OTHNVS,OTHFFC,OTHWOC,OTHPOC,UC,UCLOC,UCCRCD,UCCOMRE,UCCOMRES,UCCOMREU,UCSC,UCOTHER,UCOVER1,SCLENT,OTHOFFBS,PARTCONV,LOCFPSB,LOCFPSBK,LOCFSB,LOCFSBK,LOCPSB,LOCPSBK,LOCCOM&sort_by=REPDTE&sort_order=DESC&limit=10000&offset=0&format=json"
```

### Parameters

- **filters**: `ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930`
  - `ACTIVE:1` - Only active institutions
  - `!(BKCLASS:NC)` - Exclude non-chartered institutions
  - `REPDTE:20220930` - Report date (Q3 2022 - adjust as needed)
  
- **fields**: Comprehensive list of off-balance sheet fields including:
  - Institution identifiers (CERT, RSSDHCR, NAMEFULL, etc.)
  - **Derivatives**:
    - OBSDIR - Off-balance sheet derivatives
    - NACDIR - Notional amount of credit derivatives
    - CTDERGTY - Credit derivatives sold - guarantees
    - CTDERBEN - Credit derivatives purchased - beneficial interests
    - **Interest Rate Derivatives (RT prefix)**:
      - RT - Total interest rate derivatives
      - RTNVS - Interest rate derivatives - notional value sold
      - RTFFC - Interest rate derivatives - fair value credit exposure
      - RTWOC - Interest rate derivatives - written options contracts
      - RTPOC - Interest rate derivatives - purchased options contracts
    - **Foreign Exchange Derivatives (FX prefix)**:
      - FX - Total foreign exchange derivatives
      - FXNVS - Foreign exchange derivatives - notional value sold
      - FXFFC - Foreign exchange derivatives - fair value credit exposure
      - FXSPOT - Foreign exchange spot contracts
      - FXWOC - Foreign exchange derivatives - written options contracts
      - FXPOC - Foreign exchange derivatives - purchased options contracts
    - **Other Derivatives (EDCM, OTH prefix)**:
      - EDCM - Equity derivative contracts - notional value
      - OTHNVS - Other derivatives - notional value sold
      - OTHFFC - Other derivatives - fair value credit exposure
      - OTHWOC - Other derivatives - written options contracts
      - OTHPOC - Other derivatives - purchased options contracts
  - **Unused Commitments (UC prefix)**:
    - UC - Total unused commitments
    - UCLOC - Unused commitments - lines of credit
    - UCCRCD - Unused commitments - credit cards
    - UCCOMRE - Unused commitments - commercial real estate
    - UCCOMRES - Unused commitments - commercial real estate secured
    - UCCOMREU - Unused commitments - commercial real estate unsecured
    - UCSC - Unused commitments - standby letters of credit
    - UCOTHER - Unused commitments - other
    - UCOVER1 - Unused commitments over 1 year
  - **Other Off-Balance Sheet Items**:
    - SCLENT - Securities lending
    - OTHOFFBS - Other off-balance sheet items
    - PARTCONV - Participations in conversions
    - LOCFPSB - Letters of credit - federal public sector banks
    - LOCFSB - Letters of credit - federal savings banks
    - LOCPSB - Letters of credit - private sector banks
    - LOCCOM - Letters of credit - commercial

- **sort_by**: `REPDTE` (Report Date)
- **sort_order**: `DESC` (Descending - most recent first)
- **limit**: `10000` (Maximum records)
- **format**: `json` or `csv` (for download)

### CSV Download

To download as CSV:

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=...&format=csv&download=true&filename=off_balance_sheet_report"
```

## Key Fields Explained

### Derivatives
- **OBSDIR** - Off-balance sheet derivatives
- **NACDIR** - Notional amount of credit derivatives
- **RT** - Total interest rate derivatives
- **FX** - Total foreign exchange derivatives
- **EDCM** - Equity derivative contracts

### Unused Commitments
- **UC** - Total unused commitments
- **UCLOC** - Unused commitments - lines of credit
- **UCCRCD** - Unused commitments - credit cards
- **UCCOMRE** - Unused commitments - commercial real estate
- **UCSC** - Unused commitments - standby letters of credit
- **UCOTHER** - Unused commitments - other

### Other Off-Balance Sheet Items
- **SCLENT** - Securities lending
- **OTHOFFBS** - Other off-balance sheet items
- **LOCFPSB** - Letters of credit - federal public sector banks
- **LOCFSB** - Letters of credit - federal savings banks
- **LOCPSB** - Letters of credit - private sector banks

## Use Cases

- Risk assessment of off-balance sheet exposures
- Regulatory capital calculations
- Credit risk analysis
- Liquidity risk monitoring
- Comprehensive financial analysis

## Notes

- Off-balance sheet items represent potential future obligations
- Derivatives can create significant exposure even if not on the balance sheet
- Unused commitments represent potential future lending
- Update the `REPDTE` filter to get data for different reporting periods
- The report date format is `YYYYMMDD` (e.g., `20220930` for September 30, 2022)
- For quarterly reports, use quarter-end dates: `20220331`, `20220630`, `20220930`, `20221231`
