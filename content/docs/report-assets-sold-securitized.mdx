# Bank Assets Sold and Securitized Report

<Cards>
  <Card title="View Interactive Example" href="/example/assets-sold-securitized-report" description="See this report with live data from the FDIC API, including searchable tables and interactive filtering" />
</Cards>

This standard report provides detailed information on assets that have been sold or securitized by active FDIC-insured institutions, excluding non-chartered institutions.

## Report Components

The report includes:

- **1-4 Family Residential Loans** - Principal balance sold and securitized
- **Home Equity Lines** - Principal balance sold and securitized
- **Credit Card Receivables** - Principal balance sold and securitized
- **Auto Loans** - Principal balance sold and securitized
- **Other Consumer Loans** - Principal balance sold and securitized
- **Commercial and Industrial (C&I) Loans** - Principal balance sold and securitized
- **All Other Loans & Leases** - Principal balance sold and securitized
- **Maximum Credit Exposure** - For securitizations sponsored by other institutions
- **Assets Sold with Recourse** - 1-4 Family Residential Loans and All Other Loans
- **Memo: Bank Assets Sold and Securitized** - Additional reference information

## API Request

### Endpoint

```
GET /api/financials
```

### Example Request

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=ASSET,DEP,CERT,RSSDHCR,NAMEFULL,CITY,STALP,ZIP,REPDTE,BKCLASS,NAMEHCR,OFFDOM,SPECGRP,SUBCHAPS,ESTYMD,INSDATE,EFFDATE,MUTUAL,PARCERT,TRUST,REGAGNT,INSAGNT1,FDICDBS,FDICSUPV,FLDOFF,FED,OCCDIST,OTSREGNM,OFFOA,CB,SZLNRES,SZISLRES,SZ30RES,SZ90RES,SZDRRES,SZCRRES,SZLNHEL,SZISLHEL,SZ30HEL,SZ90HEL,SZDRHEL,SZCRHEL,SZLNCRCD,SZISLCCD,SZ30CRCD,SZ90CRCD,SZDRCRCD,SZCRCRCD,SZLAUTO,SZISLAUT,SZ30AUTO,SZ90AUTO,SZDRAUTO,SZCRAUTO,SZLNCON,SZISLCON,SZ30CON,SZ90CON,SZDRCON,SZCRCON,SZLNCI,SZISLCI,SZ30CI,SZ90CI,SZDRCI,SZCRCI,SZLNOTH,SZISLOTH,SZ30OTH,SZ90OTH,SZDROTH,SZCROTH,ENCERES,ENCEAUTO,ENCECON,ENCECI,ENCEOTH,ASDRRES,ASCERES,ASDROTH,ASCEOTH,MSRECE,MSRNRECE,LNSERV,MSRESFCL,ABCXBK,ABCXOTH,ABCUBK,ABCUOTH,SZCRCDFE&sort_by=REPDTE&sort_order=DESC&limit=10000&offset=0&format=json"
```

- **filters**: `ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930`
  - `ACTIVE:1` - Only active institutions
  - `!(BKCLASS:NC)` - Exclude non-chartered institutions
  - `REPDTE:20220930` - Report date (Q3 2022 - adjust as needed)
  
- **fields**: Comprehensive list of assets sold and securitized fields including:
  - Institution identifiers (CERT, RSSDHCR, NAMEFULL, etc.)
  - **1-4 Family Residential Loans (SZLNRES prefix)**:
    - SZLNRES - Total 1-4 family residential loans sold/securitized

## Use Cases

- Securitization activity analysis
- Risk assessment of sold assets
- Credit exposure monitoring
- Regulatory reporting for securitizations
- Portfolio management analysis

## Notes

- Assets sold and securitized represent loans removed from the balance sheet
- Recourse provisions may create ongoing risk exposure
- Update the `REPDTE` filter to get data for different reporting periods
- The report date format is `YYYYMMDD` (e.g., `20220930` for September 30, 2022)
- Maximum credit exposure represents potential future obligations
- For quarterly reports, use quarter-end dates: `20220331`, `20220630`, `20220930`, `20221231`
