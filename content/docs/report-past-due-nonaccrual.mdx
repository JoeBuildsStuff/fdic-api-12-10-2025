# Past Due and Nonaccrual Assets Report

<Cards>
  <Card title="View Interactive Example" href="/example/past-due-nonaccrual-report" description="See this report with live data from the FDIC API, including searchable tables and interactive filtering" />
</Cards>

This standard report provides detailed information on assets that are past due or in nonaccrual status for active FDIC-insured institutions, excluding non-chartered institutions.

## Report Components

The report includes:

- **Assets Past Due 30-89 Days** - Loans and other assets that are 30 to 89 days past due
- **Assets Past Due 90 or More Days** - Loans and other assets that are 90 days or more past due
- **Assets in Nonaccrual Status** - Assets that are no longer accruing interest

## API Request

### Endpoint

```
GET /api/financials
```

### Example Request

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=CERT,RSSDHCR,NAMEFULL,CITY,STALP,ZIP,REPDTE,BKCLASS,NAMEHCR,OFFDOM,SPECGRP,SUBCHAPS,ESTYMD,INSDATE,EFFDATE,MUTUAL,PARCERT,TRUST,REGAGNT,INSAGNT1,FDICDBS,FDICSUPV,FLDOFF,FED,OCCDIST,OTSREGNM,OFFOA,CB,P3ASSET,P3RE,P3RENUS,P3RELNDO,P3RECONS,P3RECNFM,P3RECNOT,P3REAG,P3RERES,P3RERSFM,P3RERSF2,P3RELOC,P3REMULT,P3RENRES,P3RENROW,P3RENROT,P3REFOR,P3DEP,P3DEPNUS,P3AG,P3CI,P3CINUS,P3CON,P3CRCD,P3AUTO,P3CONOTH,P3FG,P3OTHLN,P3LS,P3SCDEBT,P3AGSM,P3LNSALE,P3GTYPAR,P3GTY,P3GTYGNM,P9ASSET,P9RE,P9RENUS,P9RELNDO,P9RECONS,P9RECNFM,P9RECNOT,P9REAG,P9RERES,P9RERSFM,P9RERSF2,P9RELOC,P9REMULT,P9RENRES,P9RENROW,P9RENROT,P9REFOR,P9DEP,P9DEPNUS,P9AG,P9CI,P9CINUS,P9CON,P9CRCD,P9AUTO,P9CONOTH,P9FG,P9OTHLN,P9LS,P9SCDEBT,P9AGSM,P9LNSALE,P9GTYPAR,P9GTY,P9GTYGNM,NAASSET,NARE,NARENUS,NARELNDO,NARECONS,NARECNFM,NARECNOT,NAREAG,NARERES,NARERSFM,NARERSF2,NARELOC,NAREMULT,NARENRES,NARENROW,NARENROT,NAREFOR,NADEP,NADEPNUS,NAAG,NACI,NACINUS,NACON,NACRCD,NAAUTO,NACONOTH,NAFG,NAOTHLN,NALS,NASCDEBT,NAAGSM,NALNSALE,NAGTYPAR,NAGTY,NAGTYGNM&sort_by=REPDTE&sort_order=DESC&limit=10000&offset=0&format=json"
```

### Parameters

- **filters**: `ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930`
  - `ACTIVE:1` - Only active institutions
  - `!(BKCLASS:NC)` - Exclude non-chartered institutions
  - `REPDTE:20220930` - Report date (Q3 2022 - adjust as needed)
  
- **fields**: Comprehensive list of past due and nonaccrual fields including:
  - Institution identifiers (CERT, RSSDHCR, NAMEFULL, etc.)
  - **30-89 Days Past Due (P3 prefix)**:
    - P3ASSET - Total assets past due 30-89 days
    - P3RE - Real estate loans past due 30-89 days
    - P3AG - Agricultural loans past due 30-89 days
    - P3CI - Commercial and industrial loans past due 30-89 days
    - P3CON - Consumer loans past due 30-89 days
    - P3CRCD - Credit card loans past due 30-89 days
    - P3AUTO - Auto loans past due 30-89 days
    - And other P3-prefixed fields
  - **90+ Days Past Due (P9 prefix)**:
    - P9ASSET - Total assets past due 90+ days
    - P9RE - Real estate loans past due 90+ days
    - P9AG - Agricultural loans past due 90+ days
    - P9CI - Commercial and industrial loans past due 90+ days
    - And other P9-prefixed fields
  - **Nonaccrual (NA prefix)**:
    - NAASSET - Total nonaccrual assets
    - NARE - Nonaccrual real estate loans
    - NAAG - Nonaccrual agricultural loans
    - NACI - Nonaccrual commercial and industrial loans
    - And other NA-prefixed fields

- **sort_by**: `REPDTE` (Report Date)
- **sort_order**: `DESC` (Descending - most recent first)
- **limit**: `10000` (Maximum records)
- **format**: `json` or `csv` (for download)

### CSV Download


## Notes

- Update the `REPDTE` filter to get data for different reporting periods
- The report date format is `YYYYMMDD` (e.g., `20220930` for September 30, 2022)
- Past due and nonaccrual assets are key indicators of credit quality
- Compare these metrics over time to identify trends in asset quality
- For quarterly reports, use quarter-end dates: `20220331`, `20220630`, `20220930`, `20221231`
