# Performance & Condition Ratios Report

<Cards>
  <Card title="View Interactive Example" href="/example/performance-ratios-report" description="See this report with live data from the FDIC API, including searchable tables and interactive filtering" />
</Cards>

This standard report provides comprehensive performance and condition ratios for active FDIC-insured institutions, excluding non-chartered institutions. These ratios are essential for analyzing bank profitability, efficiency, asset quality, and capital adequacy.

## Report Components

The report includes the following ratio categories:

### Performance and Condition Ratios

- **Number of Institutions Reporting** - Count of institutions in the dataset
- **% of Unprofitable Institutions** - Percentage of institutions with negative net income
- **% of Institutions with Earning Gains** - Percentage of institutions with positive earnings

### Performance Ratios (%, annualized)

- **Yield on Earning Assets** - Interest income as a percentage of earning assets
- **Costs of Funding Earning Assets** - Interest expense as a percentage of earning assets
- **Net Interest Margin** - Net interest income as a percentage of earning assets
- **Noninterest Income to Average Assets** - Noninterest income as a percentage of average assets
- **Noninterest Expense to Average Assets** - Noninterest expense as a percentage of average assets
- **Credit Loss Provision to Assets** - Provision for credit losses as a percentage of assets
- **Net Operating Income to Assets** - Net operating income as a percentage of assets
- **Return on Assets (ROA)** - Net income as a percentage of average assets
- **Pretax Return on Assets** - Pretax income as a percentage of average assets
- **Return on Equity (ROE)** - Net income as a percentage of average equity
- **Retained Earnings to Average Equity** - Retained earnings as a percentage of average equity (YTD only)
- **Net Charge-Offs to Loans and Leases** - Net charge-offs as a percentage of loans and leases
- **Loan and Lease Loss Provision to Net Charge-Offs** - Provision coverage ratio
- **Earnings Coverage of Net Loan Charge-Offs** - Earnings multiple covering net charge-offs
- **Efficiency Ratio** - Noninterest expense divided by net interest income plus noninterest income
- **Assets Per Employee** - Total assets divided by number of employees ($Millions)
- **Cash Dividends to Net Income** - Cash dividends as a percentage of net income (YTD Only)

### Condition Ratios

- **Earning Assets to Total Assets** - Earning assets as a percentage of total assets
- **Loss Allowance to Loans and Leases** - Allowance for loan losses as a percentage of loans
- **Loss Allowance to Noncurrent Loans and Leases** - Allowance coverage of noncurrent loans
- **Noncurrent Assets Plus Other Real Estate Owned to Assets** - Problem assets as a percentage of total assets
- **Noncurrent Loans to Loans** - Noncurrent loans as a percentage of total loans
- **Net Loans and Leases to Assets** - Net loans as a percentage of total assets
- **Net Loans and Leases to Deposits** - Net loans as a percentage of total deposits
- **Net Loans and Leases to Core Deposits** - Net loans as a percentage of core deposits
- **Domestic Deposits to Total Assets** - Domestic deposits as a percentage of total assets
- **Equity Capital to Assets** - Total equity as a percentage of total assets
- **Leverage (Core Capital) Ratio** - Tier 1 capital as a percentage of average assets
- **Community Bank Leverage Ratio Election** - CBLR election indicator (1 = Yes)
- **Common Equity Tier 1 Capital Ratio** - CET1 capital as a percentage of risk-weighted assets
- **Tier 1 Risk-Based Capital Ratio** - Tier 1 capital as a percentage of risk-weighted assets
- **Total Risk-Based Capital Ratio** - Total capital as a percentage of risk-weighted assets

## API Request

### Endpoint

```
GET /api/financials
```

### Example Request

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=CERT,RSSDHCR,NAMEFULL,CITY,STALP,ZIP,REPDTE,BKCLASS,NAMEHCR,OFFDOM,SPECGRP,SUBCHAPS,ESTYMD,INSDATE,EFFDATE,MUTUAL,PARCERT,TRUST,REGAGNT,INSAGNT1,FDICDBS,FDICSUPV,FLDOFF,FED,OCCDIST,OTSREGNM,OFFOA,CB,NTINCL,NTINCHPP,INTINCY,INTEXPY,NIMY,NONIIAY,NONIXAY,ELNATRY,NOIJY,ROA,ROAPTX,ROE,ROEINJR,NTLNLSR,NTRER,NTRECOSR,NTRENRSR,NTREMULR,NTRERESR,NTRELOCR,NTREOTHR,IDNTCIR,IDNTCONR,IDNTCRDR,IDNTCOOR,NTAUTOPR,NTCONOTR,NTALLOTHR,NTCOMRER,ELNANTR,IDERNCVR,EEFFR,ASTEMPM,EQCDIVNTINC,ERNASTR,LNATRESR,LNRESNCR,NPERFV,NCLNLS,NCLNLSR,NCRER,NCRECONR,NCRENRER,NCREMULR,NCRERESR,NCRELOCR,NCREREOR,IDNCCIR,IDNCCONR,IDNCCRDR,IDNCCOOR,IDNCATOR,IDNCCOTR,IDNCOTHR,NCCOMRER,IDNCGTPR,LNLSNTV,LNLSDEPR,IDLNCORR,DEPDASTR,EQV,RBC1AAJ,CBLRIND,IDTICER,IDTIRWAJR,RBCRWAJ&sort_by=REPDTE&sort_order=DESC&limit=10000&offset=0&format=json"
```

### Parameters

- **filters**: `ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930`
  - `ACTIVE:1` - Only active institutions
  - `!(BKCLASS:NC)` - Exclude non-chartered institutions
  - `REPDTE:20220930` - Report date (Q3 2022 - adjust as needed)
  
- **fields**: Performance and condition ratio fields including:
  - Institution identifiers (CERT, RSSDHCR, NAMEFULL, etc.)
  - Performance ratios (INTINCY, INTEXPY, NIMY, NONIIAY, NONIXAY, ELNATRY, NOIJY, ROA, ROAPTX, ROE, ROEINJR)
  - Charge-off ratios (NTLNLSR, NTRER, IDNTCIR, IDNTCONR, IDNTCRDR, etc.)
  - Coverage ratios (ELNANTR, IDERNCVR, EEFFR)
  - Efficiency metrics (ASTEMPM, EQCDIVNTINC)
  - Asset quality ratios (ERNASTR, LNATRESR, LNRESNCR, NPERFV, NCLNLS, NCLNLSR)
  - Capital ratios (EQV, RBC1AAJ, CBLRIND, IDTICER, IDTIRWAJR, RBCRWAJ)
  - Loan-to-deposit ratios (LNLSNTV, LNLSDEPR, IDLNCORR, DEPDASTR)

- **sort_by**: `REPDTE` (Report Date)
- **sort_order**: `DESC` (Descending - most recent first)
- **limit**: `10000` (Maximum records)
- **format**: `json` or `csv` (for download)

### CSV Download

To download as CSV:

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=...&format=csv&download=true&filename=performance_ratios_report"
```

## Key Fields Explained

### Performance Ratios

- **INTINCY** - Yield on earning assets (%)
- **INTEXPY** - Costs of funding earning assets (%)
- **NIMY** - Net interest margin (%)
- **NONIIAY** - Noninterest income to average assets (%)
- **NONIXAY** - Noninterest expense to average assets (%)
- **ELNATRY** - Credit loss provision to assets (%)
- **NOIJY** - Net operating income to assets (%)
- **ROA** - Return on assets (%)
- **ROAPTX** - Pretax return on assets (%)
- **ROE** - Return on equity (%)
- **ROEINJR** - Retained earnings to average equity (%)
- **NTLNLSR** - Net charge-offs to loans and leases (%)
- **ELNANTR** - Loan and lease loss provision to net charge-offs
- **IDERNCVR** - Earnings coverage of net loan charge-offs (x)
- **EEFFR** - Efficiency ratio (%)
- **ASTEMPM** - Assets per employee ($Millions)
- **EQCDIVNTINC** - Cash dividends to net income (%)

### Condition Ratios

- **ERNASTR** - Earning assets to total assets (%)
- **LNATRESR** - Loss allowance to loans and leases (%)
- **LNRESNCR** - Loss allowance to noncurrent loans and leases (%)
- **NPERFV** - Noncurrent assets plus ORE to assets (%)
- **NCLNLS** - Noncurrent loans and leases
- **NCLNLSR** - Noncurrent loans to loans (%)
- **LNLSNTV** - Net loans and leases to assets (%)
- **LNLSDEPR** - Net loans and leases to deposits (%)
- **IDLNCORR** - Net loans and leases to core deposits (%)
- **DEPDASTR** - Domestic deposits to total assets (%)
- **EQV** - Equity capital to assets (%)
- **RBC1AAJ** - Leverage (core capital) ratio (%)
- **CBLRIND** - Community Bank Leverage Ratio Election (1 = Yes)
- **IDTICER** - Common Equity Tier 1 capital ratio (%)
- **IDTIRWAJR** - Tier 1 risk-based capital ratio (%)
- **RBCRWAJ** - Total risk-based capital ratio (%)

## Use Cases

- Bank performance benchmarking and analysis
- Profitability and efficiency evaluation
- Asset quality assessment
- Capital adequacy analysis
- Regulatory compliance monitoring
- Peer group comparisons
- Risk assessment
- Investment analysis

## Notes

- Update the `REPDTE` filter to get data for different reporting periods
- The report date format is `YYYYMMDD` (e.g., `20220930` for September 30, 2022)
- Adjust the date range in the filter to get historical comparisons
- For quarterly reports, use quarter-end dates: `20220331`, `20220630`, `20220930`, `20221231`
- Most ratios are annualized percentages
- Some ratios are only available on a year-to-date (YTD) basis
- Capital ratios are critical for regulatory compliance and should be monitored regularly
