# Assets Covered by Loss-Sharing Agreements Report

<Cards>
  <Card title="View Interactive Example" href="/example/loss-sharing-report" description="See this report with live data from the FDIC API, including searchable tables and interactive filtering" />
</Cards>

This standard report provides information on assets covered by loss-sharing agreements with the FDIC for active FDIC-insured institutions, excluding non-chartered institutions.

## Report Components

The report includes:

- **Carrying amount of covered loans and leases** - Total loans and leases covered by loss-sharing agreements
- **Carrying amount of covered loans and leases 30-89 days past due** - Covered assets that are 30-89 days past due
- **Carrying amount of covered loans and leases 90 days or more past due** - Covered assets that are 90+ days past due

## API Request

### Endpoint

```
GET /api/financials
```

### Example Request

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=CERT,RSSDHCR,NAMEFULL,CITY,STALP,ZIP,REPDTE,BKCLASS,NAMEHCR,OFFDOM,SPECGRP,SUBCHAPS,ESTYMD,INSDATE,EFFDATE,MUTUAL,PARCERT,TRUST,REGAGNT,INSAGNT1,FDICDBS,FDICSUPV,FLDOFF,FED,OCCDIST,OTSREGNM,OFFOA,CB,LSALNLS,LRECONS,LREAG,LRERES,LREMULT,LRENRES,LCI,LCON,LAG,LOTH,P3LTOT,P3LRECON,P3LREAG,P3LRERES,P3LREMUL,P3LRENRS,P3LCI,P3LCON,P3LAG,P3LOTH,P3LGTY,P9LTOT,P9LRECON,P9LREAG,P9LRERES,P9LREMUL,P9LRENRS,P9LCI,P9LCON,P9LAG,P9LOTH,P9LGTY,NALTOT,NALRECON,NALREAG,NALRERES,NALREMUL,NALRENRS,NALCI,NALCON,NALAG,NALOTH,NALGTY,LSAORE,LOREGTY,LSASCDBT,LSAOA&sort_by=REPDTE&sort_order=DESC&limit=10000&offset=0&format=json"
```

### Parameters

- **filters**: `ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930`
  - `ACTIVE:1` - Only active institutions
  - `!(BKCLASS:NC)` - Exclude non-chartered institutions
  - `REPDTE:20220930` - Report date (Q3 2022 - adjust as needed)
  
- **fields**: Comprehensive list of loss-sharing agreement fields including:
  - Institution identifiers (CERT, RSSDHCR, NAMEFULL, etc.)
  - **Total Covered Assets (LS prefix)**:
    - LSALNLS - Total loans and leases covered by loss-sharing
    - LRECONS - Covered construction loans
    - LREAG - Covered agricultural real estate loans
    - LRERES - Covered residential real estate loans
    - LREMULT - Covered multifamily real estate loans
    - LRENRES - Covered nonresidential real estate loans
    - LCI - Covered commercial and industrial loans
    - LCON - Covered consumer loans
    - LAG - Covered agricultural loans
    - LOTH - Covered other loans
  - **30-89 Days Past Due Covered (P3L prefix)**:
    - P3LTOT - Total covered assets past due 30-89 days
    - P3LRECON - Covered construction loans past due 30-89 days
    - P3LREAG - Covered agricultural real estate past due 30-89 days
    - And other P3L-prefixed fields
  - **90+ Days Past Due Covered (P9L prefix)**:
    - P9LTOT - Total covered assets past due 90+ days
    - P9LRECON - Covered construction loans past due 90+ days
    - P9LREAG - Covered agricultural real estate past due 90+ days
    - And other P9L-prefixed fields
  - **Nonaccrual Covered (NAL prefix)**:
    - NALTOT - Total nonaccrual covered assets
    - NALRECON - Nonaccrual covered construction loans
    - NALREAG - Nonaccrual covered agricultural real estate
    - And other NAL-prefixed fields
  - **Other Covered Assets**:
    - LSAORE - Covered other real estate owned
    - LSASCDBT - Covered securities debt
    - LSAOA - Covered all other assets

- **sort_by**: `REPDTE` (Report Date)
- **sort_order**: `DESC` (Descending - most recent first)
- **limit**: `10000` (Maximum records)
- **format**: `json` or `csv` (for download)

### CSV Download

To download as CSV:

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=...&format=csv&download=true&filename=loss_sharing_report"
```

## Key Fields Explained

### Total Covered Assets
- **LSALNLS** - Total loans and leases covered by loss-sharing agreements
- **LRECONS** - Covered construction loans
- **LREAG** - Covered agricultural real estate loans
- **LRERES** - Covered residential real estate loans
- **LREMULT** - Covered multifamily real estate loans
- **LRENRES** - Covered nonresidential real estate loans
- **LCI** - Covered commercial and industrial loans
- **LCON** - Covered consumer loans
- **LAG** - Covered agricultural loans
- **LOTH** - Covered other loans

### Past Due Covered Assets
- **P3LTOT** - Total covered assets past due 30-89 days
- **P9LTOT** - Total covered assets past due 90+ days
- **NALTOT** - Total nonaccrual covered assets

### Other Covered Assets
- **LSAORE** - Covered other real estate owned
- **LSASCDBT** - Covered securities debt
- **LSAOA** - Covered all other assets

## Use Cases

- Monitoring assets acquired through failed bank acquisitions
- Tracking performance of covered assets
- Regulatory reporting for loss-sharing agreements
- Risk assessment of covered portfolios
- Financial analysis of institutions with loss-sharing agreements

## Notes

- Loss-sharing agreements are typically associated with failed bank acquisitions
- Update the `REPDTE` filter to get data for different reporting periods
- The report date format is `YYYYMMDD` (e.g., `20220930` for September 30, 2022)
- These agreements provide protection against losses on covered assets
- For quarterly reports, use quarter-end dates: `20220331`, `20220630`, `20220930`, `20221231`
