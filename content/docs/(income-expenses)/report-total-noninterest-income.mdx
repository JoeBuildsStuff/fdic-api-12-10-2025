# Total Noninterest Income Report

<Cards>
  <Card title="View Interactive Example" href="/example/total-noninterest-income-report" description="See this report with live data from the FDIC API, including searchable tables and interactive filtering" />
</Cards>

This standard report provides a comprehensive breakdown of total noninterest income for active FDIC-insured institutions, excluding non-chartered institutions.

## Report Components

The report includes the following noninterest income categories:

- **Fiduciary Activities** - Income from fiduciary and trust activities
- **Service Charges on Deposit Accounts** - Fees charged on deposit accounts
- **Trading Account Gains & Fees** - Gains and fees from trading activities
- **Additional Noninterest Income** - Including:
  - Investment banking, venture capital, and merchant banking fees
  - Venture capital income
  - Service fees
  - Securities brokerage commissions
  - Insurance commissions
  - Insurance underwriting income
  - Insurance other income
  - Net gains (losses) on loans
  - Net gains (losses) on real estate
  - Net gains (losses) on assets
  - Other noninterest income

## API Request

### Endpoint

```
GET /api/financials
```

### Example Request

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=ASSET,DEP,CERT,RSSDHCR,NAMEFULL,CITY,STALP,ZIP,REPDTE,BKCLASS,NAMEHCR,OFFDOM,SPECGRP,SUBCHAPS,ESTYMD,INSDATE,EFFDATE,MUTUAL,PARCERT,TRUST,REGAGNT,INSAGNT1,FDICDBS,FDICSUPV,FLDOFF,FED,OCCDIST,OTSREGNM,OFFOA,CB,NONII,IFIDUC,ISERCHG,IGLTRAD,IGLRTEX,IGLFXEX,IGLEDEX,IGLCMEX,IGLCREX,ADDNONII,IINVFEE,IVENCAP,ISERFEE,ISECZ,IINSCOM,IINSUND,IINSOTH,NETGNSLN,NETGNSRE,NETGNAST,IOTNII&sort_by=REPDTE&sort_order=DESC&limit=10000&offset=0&format=json"
```

### Parameters

- **filters**: `ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930`
  - `ACTIVE:1` - Only active institutions
  - `!(BKCLASS:NC)` - Exclude non-chartered institutions
  - `REPDTE:20220930` - Report date (Q3 2022 - adjust as needed)
  
- **fields**: Noninterest income-related fields including:
  - Institution identifiers (CERT, RSSDHCR, NAMEFULL, etc.)
  - Total noninterest income (NONII)
  - Fiduciary activities (IFIDUC)
  - Service charges (ISERCHG)
  - Trading account gains (IGLTRAD, IGLRTEX, IGLFXEX, IGLEDEX, IGLCMEX, IGLCREX)
  - Additional noninterest income (ADDNONII, IINVFEE, IVENCAP, ISERFEE, ISECZ, IINSCOM, IINSUND, IINSOTH)
  - Net gains on loans, real estate, and assets (NETGNSLN, NETGNSRE, NETGNAST)
  - Other noninterest income (IOTNII)

- **sort_by**: `REPDTE` (Report Date)
- **sort_order**: `DESC` (Descending - most recent first)
- **limit**: `10000` (Maximum records)
- **format**: `json` or `csv` (for download)

### CSV Download

To download as CSV:

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=...&format=csv&download=true&filename=total_noninterest_income_report"
```

## Key Fields Explained

- **NONII** - Total noninterest income
- **IFIDUC** - Income from fiduciary activities
- **ISERCHG** - Service charges on deposit accounts
- **IGLTRAD** - Trading account gains and fees
- **ADDNONII** - Additional noninterest income
- **NETGNSLN** - Net gains (losses) on loans
- **NETGNSRE** - Net gains (losses) on real estate
- **NETGNAST** - Net gains (losses) on assets
- **IOTNII** - Other noninterest income

## Use Cases

- Fee income analysis and benchmarking
- Revenue diversification analysis
- Trading activity evaluation
- Regulatory reporting
- Market research

## Notes

- Update the `REPDTE` filter to get data for different reporting periods
- The report date format is `YYYYMMDD` (e.g., `20220930` for September 30, 2022)
- Adjust the date range in the filter to get historical comparisons
- For quarterly reports, use quarter-end dates: `20220331`, `20220630`, `20220930`, `20221231`
