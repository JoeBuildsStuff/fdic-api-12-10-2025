# Total Interest Income Report

<Cards>
  <Card title="View Interactive Example" href="/example/total-interest-income-report" description="See this report with live data from the FDIC API, including searchable tables and interactive filtering" />
</Cards>

This standard report provides a comprehensive breakdown of total interest income for active FDIC-insured institutions, excluding non-chartered institutions.

## Report Components

The report includes the following interest income categories:

- **Domestic Office Loans** - Interest income from loans made by domestic offices
- **Foreign Office Loans** - Interest income from loans made by foreign offices
- **Lease Financing Receivables** - Interest income from lease financing
- **Balances Due from Depository Institutions** - Interest on balances with other banks
- **Securities** - Interest income from investment securities
- **Trading Accounts** - Interest income from trading account assets
- **Federal Funds Sold** - Interest income from federal funds sold and reverse repurchase agreements
- **Other Interest Income** - Miscellaneous interest income not categorized elsewhere

## API Request

### Endpoint

```
GET /api/financials
```

### Example Request

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=ASSET,DEP,CERT,RSSDHCR,NAMEFULL,CITY,STALP,ZIP,REPDTE,BKCLASS,NAMEHCR,OFFDOM,SPECGRP,SUBCHAPS,ESTYMD,INSDATE,EFFDATE,MUTUAL,PARCERT,TRUST,REGAGNT,INSAGNT1,FDICDBS,FDICSUPV,FLDOFF,FED,OCCDIST,OTSREGNM,OFFOA,CB,INTINC,ILNDOM,ILNFOR,ILS,ICHBAL,ISC,ITRADE,IFREPO,IOTHII&sort_by=REPDTE&sort_order=DESC&limit=10000&offset=0&format=json"
```

### Parameters

- **filters**: `ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930`
  - `ACTIVE:1` - Only active institutions
  - `!(BKCLASS:NC)` - Exclude non-chartered institutions
  - `REPDTE:20220930` - Report date (Q3 2022 - adjust as needed)
  
- **fields**: Interest income-related fields including:
  - Institution identifiers (CERT, RSSDHCR, NAMEFULL, etc.)
  - Total interest income (INTINC)
  - Domestic office loans (ILNDOM)
  - Foreign office loans (ILNFOR)
  - Lease financing (ILS)
  - Balances due from depository institutions (ICHBAL)
  - Securities (ISC)
  - Trading accounts (ITRADE)
  - Federal funds sold (IFREPO)
  - Other interest income (IOTHII)

- **sort_by**: `REPDTE` (Report Date)
- **sort_order**: `DESC` (Descending - most recent first)
- **limit**: `10000` (Maximum records)
- **format**: `json` or `csv` (for download)

### CSV Download

To download as CSV:

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=...&format=csv&download=true&filename=total_interest_income_report"
```

## Key Fields Explained

- **INTINC** - Total interest income
- **ILNDOM** - Interest income from domestic office loans
- **ILNFOR** - Interest income from foreign office loans
- **ILS** - Interest income from lease financing receivables
- **ICHBAL** - Interest income from balances due from depository institutions
- **ISC** - Interest income from securities
- **ITRADE** - Interest income from trading accounts
- **IFREPO** - Interest income from federal funds sold and reverse repurchase agreements
- **IOTHII** - Other interest income

## Use Cases

## Notes

- Update the `REPDTE` filter to get data for different reporting periods
- The report date format is `YYYYMMDD` (e.g., `20220930` for September 30, 2022)
- Adjust the date range in the filter to get historical comparisons
- For quarterly reports, use quarter-end dates: `20220331`, `20220630`, `20220930`, `20221231`
