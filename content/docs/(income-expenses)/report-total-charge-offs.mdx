# Total Charge-Offs Report

<Cards>
  <Card title="View Interactive Example" href="/example/total-charge-offs-report" description="See this report with live data from the FDIC API, including searchable tables and interactive filtering" />
</Cards>

This standard report provides a comprehensive breakdown of total charge-offs by loan category for active FDIC-insured institutions, excluding non-chartered institutions.

## Report Components

The report includes total charge-offs for the following loan categories:

- **Loans Secured by Real Estate, Total** - All real estate secured loans
- **Loans to Depository Institutions** - Loans to other banks and depository institutions
- **Loans to Finance Agricultural Production and Other Loans to Farmers** - Agricultural loans
- **Commercial and Industrial Loans** - Business loans
- **Loans to Individuals** - Consumer loans including credit cards, auto loans, and other personal loans
- **Loans to Foreign Governments and Official Institutions** - International loans
- **Other Loans** - Miscellaneous loans not categorized elsewhere
- **Lease Financing Receivables** - Charge-offs on leases

## API Request

### Endpoint

```
GET /api/financials
```

### Example Request

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=CERT,RSSDHCR,NAMEFULL,CITY,STALP,ZIP,REPDTE,BKCLASS,NAMEHCR,OFFDOM,SPECGRP,SUBCHAPS,ESTYMD,INSDATE,EFFDATE,MUTUAL,PARCERT,TRUST,REGAGNT,INSAGNT1,FDICDBS,FDICSUPV,FLDOFF,FED,OCCDIST,OTSREGNM,OFFOA,CB,DRLNLS,DRRE,DRRENUS,DRREOFFDOM,DRRECONS,DRREAG,DRRERES,DRRERSFM,DRRERSF2,DRRELOC,DRREMULT,DRRENRES,DRREFOR,DRDEP,DRDEPNUS,DRAG,DRCI,DRCINUS,DRCON,DRCRCD,DRAUTO,DRCONOTH,DRFORGV,DROTHER,DRLS,DRAGSM&sort_by=REPDTE&sort_order=DESC&limit=10000&offset=0&format=json"
```

### Parameters

- **filters**: `ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930`
  - `ACTIVE:1` - Only active institutions
  - `!(BKCLASS:NC)` - Exclude non-chartered institutions
  - `REPDTE:20220930` - Report date (Q3 2022 - adjust as needed)
  
- **fields**: Total charge-off fields including:
  - Institution identifiers (CERT, RSSDHCR, NAMEFULL, etc.)
  - Total charge-offs (DRLNLS)
  - Real estate loans (DRRE, DRRENUS, DRREOFFDOM, DRRECONS, DRREAG, DRRERES, DRRERSFM, DRRERSF2, DRRELOC, DRREMULT, DRRENRES, DRREFOR)
  - Loans to depository institutions (DRDEP, DRDEPNUS)
  - Agricultural loans (DRAG, DRAGSM)
  - Commercial and industrial loans (DRCI, DRCINUS)
  - Consumer loans (DRCON, DRCRCD, DRAUTO, DRCONOTH)
  - Foreign government loans (DRFORGV)
  - Other loans (DROTHER)
  - Lease financing (DRLS)

- **sort_by**: `REPDTE` (Report Date)
- **sort_order**: `DESC` (Descending - most recent first)
- **limit**: `10000` (Maximum records)
- **format**: `json` or `csv` (for download)

### CSV Download

To download as CSV:

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=...&format=csv&download=true&filename=total_charge_offs_report"
```

## Key Fields Explained

- **DRLNLS** - Total charge-offs on loans and leases
- **DRRE** - Charge-offs on real estate loans
- **DRDEP** - Charge-offs on loans to depository institutions
- **DRAG** - Charge-offs on agricultural loans
- **DRCI** - Charge-offs on commercial and industrial loans
- **DRCON** - Charge-offs on consumer loans
- **DRCRCD** - Charge-offs on credit card loans
- **DRAUTO** - Charge-offs on auto loans
- **DRFORGV** - Charge-offs on loans to foreign governments
- **DROTHER** - Charge-offs on other loans
- **DRLS** - Charge-offs on lease financing receivables

## Use Cases

- Credit risk analysis and benchmarking
- Loan loss trend analysis
- Portfolio quality assessment
- Regulatory reporting
- Risk management

## Notes

- Update the `REPDTE` filter to get data for different reporting periods
- The report date format is `YYYYMMDD` (e.g., `20220930` for September 30, 2022)
- Adjust the date range in the filter to get historical comparisons
- For quarterly reports, use quarter-end dates: `20220331`, `20220630`, `20220930`, `20221231`
- Charge-offs represent loans written off as uncollectible during the reporting period
