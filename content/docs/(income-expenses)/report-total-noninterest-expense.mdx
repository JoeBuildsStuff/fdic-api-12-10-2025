# Total Noninterest Expense & Other Report

<Cards>
  <Card title="View Interactive Example" href="/example/total-noninterest-expense-report" description="See this report with live data from the FDIC API, including searchable tables and interactive filtering" />
</Cards>

This standard report provides a comprehensive breakdown of total noninterest expense and other income statement items for active FDIC-insured institutions, excluding non-chartered institutions.

## Report Components

The report includes the following categories:

- **Salaries and Employee Benefits** - Compensation and benefits expense
- **Premises and Equipment Expense** - Occupancy and equipment costs
- **Additional Noninterest Expense** - Other operating expenses
- **Pre-tax Net Operating Income** - Income before taxes and extraordinary items
- **Securities Gains (Losses)** - Gains or losses on sale of securities
- **Applicable Income Taxes** - Income tax expense
- **Income Before Extraordinary Items** - Net income before extraordinary items
- **Discontinued Operations (Extraordinary Gains - Net)** - Extraordinary items and discontinued operations
- **Net Income Attributable to Bank and Noncontrolling Interests** - Total net income
- **Net Income Attributable to Noncontrolling Interests** - Noncontrolling interest portion
- **Net Income Attributable to Bank** - Net income attributable to the bank

## API Request

### Endpoint

```
GET /api/financials
```

### Example Request

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=CERT,RSSDHCR,NAMEFULL,CITY,STALP,ZIP,REPDTE,BKCLASS,NAMEHCR,OFFDOM,SPECGRP,SUBCHAPS,ESTYMD,INSDATE,EFFDATE,MUTUAL,PARCERT,TRUST,REGAGNT,INSAGNT1,FDICDBS,FDICSUPV,FLDOFF,FED,OCCDIST,OTSREGNM,OFFOA,CB,NONIX,ESAL,EPREMAGG,ADDNONINTEXP,EAMINTAN,EINTOTH,EINTGW,EOTHNINT,PTAXNETINC,IGLSEC,ITAX,IBEFXTR,EXTRA,NETINBM,NETIMIN,NETINC&sort_by=REPDTE&sort_order=DESC&limit=10000&offset=0&format=json"
```

### Parameters

- **filters**: `ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930`
  - `ACTIVE:1` - Only active institutions
  - `!(BKCLASS:NC)` - Exclude non-chartered institutions
  - `REPDTE:20220930` - Report date (Q3 2022 - adjust as needed)
  
- **fields**: Noninterest expense and income statement fields including:
  - Institution identifiers (CERT, RSSDHCR, NAMEFULL, etc.)
  - Total noninterest expense (NONIX)
  - Salaries and benefits (ESAL)
  - Premises and equipment (EPREMAGG)
  - Additional noninterest expense (ADDNONINTEXP, EAMINTAN, EINTOTH, EINTGW, EOTHNINT)
  - Pre-tax net operating income (PTAXNETINC)
  - Securities gains/losses (IGLSEC)
  - Income taxes (ITAX)
  - Income before extraordinary items (IBEFXTR)
  - Extraordinary items (EXTRA)
  - Net income items (NETINBM, NETIMIN, NETINC)

- **sort_by**: `REPDTE` (Report Date)
- **sort_order**: `DESC` (Descending - most recent first)
- **limit**: `10000` (Maximum records)
- **format**: `json` or `csv` (for download)

### CSV Download

To download as CSV:

```bash
curl "https://api.fdic.gov/banks/api/financials?filters=ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930&fields=...&format=csv&download=true&filename=total_noninterest_expense_report"
```

## Key Fields Explained

- **NONIX** - Total noninterest expense
- **ESAL** - Salaries and employee benefits
- **EPREMAGG** - Premises and equipment expense
- **ADDNONINTEXP** - Additional noninterest expense
- **PTAXNETINC** - Pre-tax net operating income
- **IGLSEC** - Securities gains (losses)
- **ITAX** - Applicable income taxes
- **IBEFXTR** - Income before extraordinary items
- **EXTRA** - Discontinued operations and extraordinary items
- **NETINC** - Net income attributable to bank

## Use Cases

- Operating expense analysis and benchmarking
- Profitability analysis
- Efficiency ratio evaluation
- Regulatory reporting
- Financial performance assessment

## Notes

- Update the `REPDTE` filter to get data for different reporting periods
- The report date format is `YYYYMMDD` (e.g., `20220930` for September 30, 2022)
- Adjust the date range in the filter to get historical comparisons
- For quarterly reports, use quarter-end dates: `20220331`, `20220630`, `20220930`, `20221231`
