# Fiduciary and Related Services Report

<Cards>
  <Card title="View Interactive Example" href="/example/fiduciary-report" description="See this report with live data from the FDIC API, including searchable tables and interactive filtering" />
</Cards>

This standard report provides comprehensive information on fiduciary and related services for active FDIC-insured institutions, excluding non-chartered institutions.

## Report Components

The report includes:

- **Number with Fiduciary Powers Granted** - Institutions with fiduciary powers
- **Number with Fiduciary Power Exercised** - Institutions actively exercising fiduciary powers
- **Number with Fiduciary or Related Activity** - Institutions with fiduciary or related activity
- **Number of Fiduciary Accounts and Related Asset Accounts** - Total account counts
- **Total Fiduciary and Related Assets** - Total assets under management
- **Fiduciary and Related Services Income (YTD Only)** - Year-to-date income from fiduciary services

### Parameters

- **filters**: `ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930`
  - `ACTIVE:1` - Only active institutions
  - `!(BKCLASS:NC)` - Exclude non-chartered institutions
  - `REPDTE:20220930` - Report date (Q3 2022 - adjust as needed)
  
- **fields**: Comprehensive list of fiduciary and related services fields including:
  - Institution identifiers (CERT, RSSDHCR, NAMEFULL, etc.)
  - **Fiduciary Powers and Activity**:
    - TRPOWER - Fiduciary powers granted (1=yes, 0=no)
    - TREXER - Fiduciary powers exercised (1=yes, 0=no)
    - TRACT - Fiduciary or related activity (1=yes, 0=no)
  - **Fiduciary Assets (TFRA, TTMA, TPMA, etc.)**:
    - TFRA - Total fiduciary and related assets
    - TTMA - Total managed assets
    - TPMA - Personal managed assets
    - TECMA - Employee benefit managed assets
    - TEBMA - Employee benefit managed assets (alternative)
    - TORMA - Other managed assets
    - TCAMA - Corporate agency managed assets
    - TIMMA - Investment management managed assets
    - TFEMA - Foundation/endowment managed assets
    - TOFMA - Other fiduciary managed assets
    - TMAF - Total managed assets - fiduciary
    - TRHMA - Retirement health managed assets
    - TMASMF - Total managed assets - self-managed funds
  - **Fiduciary Accounts (NUM suffix)**:
    - TTNANUM - Total trust accounts number
    - TPMANUM - Personal managed accounts number
    - TECMANUM - Employee benefit managed accounts number
    - And other NUM-suffixed fields for account counts
  - **Fiduciary Income (TPI, TEI, TOI prefixes)**:
    - TPIMATOT - Personal investment management assets total
    - TPINI - Personal investment management income
    - TPII - Personal investment management income (alternative)
    - TEMATOT - Employee benefit investment management assets total
    - TENI - Employee benefit investment management income
    - TEI - Employee benefit investment management income (alternative)
    - TOMATOT - Other investment management assets total
    - TONI - Other investment management income
    - TOI - Other investment management income (alternative)
  - **Other Fiduciary Items**:
    - IFIDUC - Income from fiduciary activities
    - TETOT - Total employee benefit assets
    - TNL - Trust net loans
    - TINTRA - Trust intra-bank transactions
    - TNI - Trust net income
    - TITOTF - Total investment trust assets - fiduciary

- **sort_by**: `REPDTE` (Report Date)
- **sort_order**: `DESC` (Descending - most recent first)
- **limit**: `10000` (Maximum records)
- **format**: `json` or `csv` (for download)

## Key Fields Explained

### Fiduciary Powers
- **TRPOWER** - Fiduciary powers granted (1=yes, 0=no)
- **TREXER** - Fiduciary powers exercised (1=yes, 0=no)
- **TRACT** - Fiduciary or related activity (1=yes, 0=no)

### Fiduciary Assets
- **TFRA** - Total fiduciary and related assets
- **TTMA** - Total managed assets
- **TPMA** - Personal managed assets
- **TECMA** - Employee benefit managed assets
- **TORMA** - Other managed assets
- **TCAMA** - Corporate agency managed assets

### Fiduciary Accounts
- **TTNANUM** - Total trust accounts number
- **TPMANUM** - Personal managed accounts number
- **TECMANUM** - Employee benefit managed accounts number

### Fiduciary Income
- **IFIDUC** - Income from fiduciary activities
- **TPINI** - Personal investment management income
- **TENI** - Employee benefit investment management income
- **TONI** - Other investment management income

## Use Cases

- Trust department analysis
- Fiduciary services market research
- Revenue analysis from fiduciary activities
- Asset under management tracking
- Regulatory compliance for trust activities

## Notes

- Fiduciary services include trust, investment management, and related services
- Update the `REPDTE` filter to get data for different reporting periods
- The report date format is `YYYYMMDD` (e.g., `20220930` for September 30, 2022)
- Income fields (IFIDUC, TPINI, etc.) are year-to-date only
- For quarterly reports, use quarter-end dates: `20220331`, `20220630`, `20220930`, `20221231`
