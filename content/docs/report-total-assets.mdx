# Total Assets Report

  <Card title="View Interactive Example" href="/example/total-assets-report" description="See this report with live data from the FDIC API, including searchable tables and interactive filtering" />
</Cards>

This standard report provides a comprehensive breakdown of total assets for active FDIC-insured institutions, excluding non-chartered institutions.

## Report Components

The report includes the following asset categories:

- **Cash and Balances due from Depository Institutions** - Cash on hand and balances with other banks
- **Securities** - Investment securities held by the institution
- **Federal Funds Sold & Reverse Repurchase Agreements** - Short-term lending to other institutions
- **Net Loans and Leases** - Total loans and leases net of unearned income and allowances
- **Trading Account Assets** - Assets held for trading purposes
- **Bank Premises and Fixed Assets** - Physical property and equipment
- **Other Real Estate Owned (ORE)** - Foreclosed real estate properties
- **Goodwill and Other Intangibles** - Intangible assets including goodwill
- **All Other Assets** - Miscellaneous assets not categorized elsewhere

### Parameters

- **filters**: `ACTIVE:1 AND !(BKCLASS:NC) AND REPDTE:20220930`
  - `ACTIVE:1` - Only active institutions
  - `!(BKCLASS:NC)` - Exclude non-chartered institutions
  - `REPDTE:20220930` - Report date (Q3 2022 - adjust as needed)
  
- **fields**: Comprehensive list of asset-related fields including:
  - Institution identifiers (CERT, RSSDHCR, NAMEFULL, etc.)
  - Cash and balances (CHBAL, CHBALNI, CHBALI, etc.)
  - Securities (SC, SCUS, SCUST, etc.)
  - Loans and leases (LNLSNET, LNRE, LNAG, LNCI, etc.)
  - Trading assets (TRADE, TRREVALSUM)
  - Bank premises (BKPREM)
  - Other real estate owned (ORE, OREINV, etc.)
  - Intangibles (INTAN, INTANGW, etc.)
  - All other assets (AOA)

- **sort_by**: `REPDTE` (Report Date)
- **sort_order**: `DESC` (Descending - most recent first)
- **limit**: `10000` (Maximum records)
- **format**: `json` or `csv` (for download)

## Key Fields Explained

- **ASSET** - Total assets
- **CHBAL** - Cash and balances due from depository institutions
- **SC** - Total securities
- **FREPO** - Federal funds sold and reverse repurchase agreements
- **LNLSNET** - Net loans and leases
- **TRADE** - Trading account assets
- **BKPREM** - Bank premises and fixed assets
- **ORE** - Other real estate owned
- **INTAN** - Goodwill and other intangibles
- **AOA** - All other assets

## Use Cases

- Financial analysis and benchmarking
- Regulatory reporting

## Notes

- Update the `REPDTE` filter to get data for different reporting periods
- The report date format is `YYYYMMDD` (e.g., `20220930` for September 30, 2022)
- Adjust the date range in the filter to get historical comparisons
- For quarterly reports, use quarter-end dates: `20220331`, `20220630`, `20220930`, `20221231`
